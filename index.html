Welcome

A premier tour and conservation employer, built for talent that protects the wild.

Mara Horizon Trust is a modern online recruitment platform designed to simplify hiring for both job seekers and employers in the wildlife and conservation sector — supporting roles from rangers and drivers to accountants and conservation specialists, with every application tracked from submission to offer.
